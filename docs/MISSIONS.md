# Campaign missions

Five hand-authored missions. Walkthrough hints below — no full solutions,
just enough to know what you're walking into.

## Mission 1 — First Contact

- **Map**: 21×15. River chokepoint at q=8 (bridge at r=7). Mountain ridge
  north blocks the easy path.
- **You**: NATO. HQ at (2,3). Starting force: 3 infantry/recon.
- **Enemy**: BRICS, HQ at (18,11). Balanced AI personality.
- **Win**: Destroy BRICS HQ.
- **Strategy**: Build one engineer turn 1 to grab the midfield city. Push
  a vehicle + infantry pair down the southern road. The northern mountain
  approach is mostly a feint.

## Mission 2 — Shadow War

- **Map**: 20×13. Urban + forest mix.
- **You**: NATO vs Guerilla. The guerilla AI uses stealth scouts and
  mortar.
- **Win**: Hold both urban zones (cities at (8,5) and (12,8)) for 8
  consecutive turns **OR** destroy guerilla HQ.
- **Strategy**: Hold-tiles is the easier path. Build infantry, walk into
  both cities, hunker (`H`) every turn. The mortar will chip; counter-fire
  back when you can.

## Mission 3 — Iron Fist

- **Map**: 22×14. River choke at q=13 with a single bridge. 5 neutral oil
  wells scattered midfield.
- **You**: NATO vs BRICS (6 starting units — harder).
- **Win**: Own all 5 oil wells simultaneously.
- **Strategy**: Pure capture race. Build 3+ engineers fast. The bridge is
  the front line; whoever loses it loses the map.

## Mission 4 — Last Stand

- **Map**: 16×12 — compact. Forest cover + mountain funnel.
- **You**: NATO defending with only 4 units and 300 credits. BRICS blitz
  with 6 units and 1200 credits.
- **Win**: Hold your HQ for 20 turns **OR** destroy BRICS HQ.
- **Strategy**: Forget the offensive condition — survive. Hunker
  everything on cover tiles. Place AA early — BRICS will helo-rush. Build
  cheap infantry to replace losses; never let the HQ neighbour hexes go
  empty.

## Mission 5 — Decapitation

- **Map**: 24×15 (largest). Two river crossings, mountain spine center.
- **You**: NATO assault. BRICS has guarded their T3 Iskander-M flagship.
- **Win**: Destroy the named unit `brics_hypersonic` at (19,9).
- **Strategy**: The Iskander is guarded by 2× AA + vehicles. Air-rush gets
  shredded; jets must take out the AA first or you'll trade unfavourably.
  Heavy artillery (NATO T2) at long range is the cleanest approach. Don't
  bother capturing — kill the missile, win the war.
